Timex : Timex is a renowned American watch brand known for its durability, affordability, and classic designs. Established in the 1850s, it has a rich heritage in watchmaking. Timex gained popularity with its iconic and accessible timepieces, especially the Timex Ironman series, a favorite among athletes for its sports-focused features like stopwatch, alarms, and water resistance.

Sonata : The Sonata brand is an Indian watch manufacturer known for its wide range of affordable and stylish timepieces. Established in 1986, it has become one of the largest-selling watch brands in India. Sonata watches cater to various segments, offering a diverse collection encompassing analog, digital, and hybrid watches for men, women, and kids.

G-Shock : G-Shock watches are a line of tough, durable timepieces produced by Casio. Launched in 1983, they were designed to withstand shock, vibration, and extreme conditions. They're known for their robust construction, including shock-resistant materials, protective features like reinforced cases, and water resistance. G-Shock watches often incorporate various functionalities like countdown timers, stopwatch features, alarms, and world time displays.
